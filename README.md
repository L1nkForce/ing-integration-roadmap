## 🎯 Purpose

As IN-Gauge scales, integration strategy must remain deliberate and aligned. This repository exists to:

- 🗂️ Maintain a **single, version-controlled source of truth**
- 🤝 Align Sales, Product, Integrations, and Cloud Operations around shared priorities
- 🔍 Provide visibility into prioritization decisions and their rationale
- 🏆 Track certification and partnership milestones
- 📐 Ensure roadmap discipline evolves in step with the business

> 💡 This is a **living document** — it changes as the business changes.
